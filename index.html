About Me

   My name is Erik Zorn and I am from a small beach town on the coast of Maryland called Ocean City. I have always had a passion for the maths and sciences, whether it be in or out of schoolwork. Growing up I was exposed to a lot of important problem solving skills though my older brother and father who both studied engineering in college. Along with their influences and my own desire, I am now studying Computer Science in the Honors College at the University of South Carolina. Between college level coursework, projects of my own, and a summer internship in the field I have developed a greater appreciation of computing. As I progress toward more advanced CS, I am beginning to find great satisfaction in optimizing code efficiency and elegancy. Gaining knowlege in computer architecture has made these properties more meaningful to me. I look forward to developing as a programmer in the next few years, and I am very excited about what the future has to offer.
